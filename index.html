Some facts about tigers:

  • India is the country with the largest number of wild tigers.
  • Tiger cubs are born blind, leading to a 50% survival rate.
  • Tiger tongues have sharp, teeth-like projections called papilae.
  • Siberian tigers are the largest species of tigers on this planet.

Some more fun facts about tigers:

  1. Tigers use their ears to communicate, with an example being a mom flattening her ears to warn her cubs of danger.
  2. Because only 1 in 10 hunts are successful, Tigers only eat about once per week.
  3. At full speed, Tigers can reach up to 65 km/hr.
